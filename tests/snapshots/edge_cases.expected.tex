\section{Accent Commands}
LaTeX accents: \"{o}, \'{e}, \`{a}, \^{i}, \~{n}, \c{c}, \H{o}, \u{a}, \v{s}, \t{oo}.
Also: \oe, \ae, \AA, \l, \L, \O, \o, \ss, \i, \j.

\section{Empty Group}
An empty group: {} and math mode: ${}$.

\section{Special Group Content}
Group with just spaces: { }.
Group with command: {\textbf{inner}}.
Group with math: {$x + y$}.

\section{Consecutive Brace Completion}
\frac{1}{2} and \frac{3}{4} in one line.
$\frac{5}{6}\frac{7}{8}$ consecutively.
$\frac{1}{2}$ with spaces.

\section{Nested Fraction Shorthand}
\frac{\frac{1}{2}}{\frac{3}{4}}.

\section{Math Space Commands}
In math: $a\,b\;c\:d\!e$ and $a\quad b$ and $a\qquad b$.
